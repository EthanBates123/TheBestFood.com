Breakfast

Pancakes

Ingrediants:

2 flour
2 Tbsp. brown sugar
1 Tbsp. baking powder
1 tsp. salt
1 ½ cups milk
2 eggs
3 ½ Tbsp. butter
1 ½ tsp. vanilla extract

Steps:

Step 1:
Combine flour, brown sugar, baking powder, and salt in a mixing bowl.

Step 2:
Mix milk, egg, vanilla, and butter into the powder mixture and mix until only small clumps remain.

Step 3:
Heat a pan on meduim high heat and once hot pour batter onto it in small amounts.
